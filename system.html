How the System Works

Our movie ticket booking system is designed to make your experience simple and efficient. Here's how it works:

  1. Select a Movie: Browse through our list of available movies and choose the one you want to watch.
  2. Pick a Screen: Select your preferred screen from the available options.
  3. Choose Your Seats: View the real-time seat layout and pick your desired seats.
  4. Confirm Your Booking: Review your selection, view the total price, and confirm your booking.
  5. Enjoy the Show: Arrive at the theater and enjoy your movie!

Our system ensures a smooth and secure booking process, so you can focus on enjoying your favorite movies.
